In The Art Of Wellington


A New Face.

In The Art Of Wellington is an augmented reality concept sprouted from the idea to make static street art come to life and last longer through digital means. I is a way for people become more appreciative and connected with local and international artists who spend tireless hours creating amazing work to make our Wellington beautiful.
